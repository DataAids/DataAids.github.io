Our Mission

DataAids uses data science to understand social problems and develop solutions. We gather and process information from various sources such as surveys and government databases. With this data, we develop tools and training to help marginalized communities with healthcare, education, and job opportunities. We aim to make a positive difference, promote fairness, and empower underserved groups.

Our Values
